Tuna Loaf

Ingredients
200 g Canned Tuna
2 eggs
1 Red Pepper
4 Spring Onions
5 cm Cucumber
1 EL Tomato Purée
2 EL Worcestershire sauce
2 EL Lemon Juice
Salt, Pepper

Preparation:

Take a bowl and put into it finely chopped pepper, onions and cucumber.

Flake drained tuna, finely chop hard boiled eggs and add to bowl.

Add lemon juice and worcestershire sauce. Salt and pepper. Mix well.

Press mixture into a pound loaf tin and chill for about 2 hours.

Turn out and garnish with cucumber and lemon. Serve in thick slices.

Source:

Herbert Eigenmann, Code: TLF

Valuation:

Nice sort of tuna vegetable salad. RE, 19.10.2004
