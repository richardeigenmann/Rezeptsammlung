Curried Nut Roast

Ingredients:
2 medium onions
1 medium green pepper
Oil for frying
225 g (8 oz) mixed nuts
85 g (3 oz) wholemeal breadcrumbs
2 cloves garlic
1 Tablespoon mixed herbs
1 Spoon medium curry powder
425 g (15 oz) tin chopped tomatoes
Salt and Pepper
1 egg

Mise en place:

Finely chop onions

Finely chop green pepper

Mince nuts

Cut bread into breadcrumbs

Peal garlic

Peal tomatoes if fresh and cut into pieces

Beat egg

Grease pie dish

Preheat Oven to 220°

Preparation:

Preparation Time: 20 Minutes, Cooking Time: 40 Minutes

Fry chopped onions in a little oil until softened.

Add finely chopped green pepper and cook for a few minutes more.

Mix nuts with onion, pepper mixture. Add breadcrumbs, then crushed garlic, herbs and curry powder. Stir in tomatoes, mix well and season with salt and pepper.

Add beaten egg to bind, and put in a greased pie dish and bake at 220° until golden.

Comments:

Serve hot or cold.

This is a very tasty dish, and makes a very satifying meal served with a salad. It freezes well and you can make double quantities and put one in the freezer before it has been cooked.
