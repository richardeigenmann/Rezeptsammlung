Cream Cheese Brownies

Ingredients:
1 cup / 220 g butter or margarine, softened
120 g unsweetened chocolate
2.5 cups / 500 g sugar
4 Eggs
1 cup / 120 g all purpose flour
0.5 Tsp salt
1 cup / 120 g chopped walnuts
2 Tsp vanilla
1 pkg (8 oz) / 225 g cream cheese sifted

Preparation:

Melt butter and chocolate in double boiler over boiling water. Remove from heat and set aside.

Place 2 cups sugar and 3 eggs in bowl. Attach bowl and flat beater. Turn to speed 4 and gradually add chocolate mixture, beating until well blended. Stop and scrape bowl.

Add flour, salt, walnuts, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Turn to Speed 2 and beat 1 minute, until well blended. remove from bowl and set aside.

Place remaining sugar, egg, vanilla, and cream cheese in clean, second bowl. Attach bowl and flat beater. Turn to speed 2 and beat 30 seconds. Stop and scrape bowl. Turn to Speed 6 and beat 1 minute.

Drop chocolate and cream cheese batter by tablespoonfuls into a greased and floured 9x13x2 inch pan. Swirl lightly with a knife. Bake at 350 ° Fahrenheid / 180°C for 45 to 50 minutes.

When cool, cut into squares.

Yield: 24 2x2 inch brownies.

Source:

Konrad Hofstetter W&A, Dec 1994

Valuation:

Excellent brownies. For my brownies pan which is a little smaller than recommended I need to add 10 minutes to the baking time. RE, 28 Feb 2010
