Tzatsiki

Ingreedients for 4 people:
1/2 cucumber
1 cup full cream Greek yoghurt
2 teaspoons fresh, mint
lemon juice
2 cloves garlic
Salt, black pepper

Preparation:

Dice the cucumber into little cubes about 0.5 cm by 0.5.

Combine the cucumber and the yoghurt in a mixing bowl.

Chop and add the mint, squeeze the lemon juice and crush fresh garlic.

Stir well and season.

Pour the mixture into a serving dish and serve with toasted pita bread slices.

Source:

Recipe Booklet from Checkers, Tastes of the World 2005
