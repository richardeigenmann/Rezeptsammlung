Danish Apple Desert

Ingredients:
8 Servings 4 Servings 1 Serving
8 4 1 Apples
1/4 cup = 60ml 2 tablespoons 1/2 tablespoon Sugar
2 tablespoons 1 tablespoon 1/4 tablespoon = 3.7 ml Butter
1/3 cup = 80 ml 0.5 dl 10 ml Water
3 2 1 Eggs
3 tablespoons 1.5 tablespoons 1/4 tablespoons Flour
3 tablespooons 1.5 tablespooons 1/4 tablespoons Sugar
1 cup = 236 ml 1.2 dl 1/8 cup = 30 ml Cream
Cinnamon
Whipping Cream

Preparation:

1 hour 25 minutes 35 mins prep

Preheat oven to 350F = 176C.

Core the apples, peel them and slice them.

Make a syrup of the sugar, butter and water by heating a pot over the stove but don't let syrup bubble (boil).

Cook the apples in the syrup until tender.

Place in a buttered 9x13 baking dish.

Beat eggs until creamy.

Add the flour and sugar and mix well.

Add the cream slowly in mix.

Pour mixture over the apples.

Bake for 50 minutes or until it is set.

Cover the baking dish with waxed paper during most of the baking to prevent browning too much.

Serve topped with whipped cream and cinnamon.
