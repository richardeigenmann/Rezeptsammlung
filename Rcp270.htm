Peach Crumble Squares

Ingredients for 9 people:
8 peaches medium-sized ripe
1 square Pie paste
1/4 cup / 50g sugar granulated
1 TL cornstarch
1 cup / 115g flour, sifted
1/2 cup / 100g brown sugar
1/8 TL cinnamon
1/3 cup / 75g butter or margarine

Zubereitung:

Roll out pie paste.

Line bottom und sides of an 20 cm square cake pan with rolled out pie paste.

Preheat oven to 220 ° (hot).

Wash, peel, stone und slice peaches, arrange over pastry.

Sprinkle with a mixture of the granulated sugar und corn starch.

Combine pastry or all-purpose flour, brown sugar und cinnamon; cut butter in finely.

Sprinkle this mixture over fruit.

Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.

Reduce oven temperature to 190 ° (moderately hot) und continue to bake the pudding 35 minutes.
