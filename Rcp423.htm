Brownies (Variante 3)

Ingrediens:
225 g dark Chocolate chips
325 g dark Chocolate to melt
225 g Butter
3 Eggs
3 cl strong Coffee
1 Stick Vanilla
75 g Flower
1/2 Tsp Salt
175 g Pecan or Walnut
325 g trickle Sugar (sticky sugar)

Mise en place:

Put ingredients ready

Break Chocolate to pea sized pieces.

Scrape grains out of vanilla stick.

Preparation:

Melt 325 g of chocolate with butter in a Bain Marie (= Wasserbad) (= bowl in the water that is boiling in a pot on the stove).

Whip eggs with coffee and sugar

Put melted butter and chocolate in egg mix.

Add vanilla grains

Put flower and salt in the mixture

At the end add the wlanuts and chocolate pieces

Bake at 190 Degrees for 30-40 minutes.

Source:

Andrea Berther from Anders and Tine Hein Mortensen
