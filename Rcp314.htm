Baked Tuna Casserole

Zutaten für 4 Personen:
200 g canned Tuna Fish
2 Tbl Butter
1 small Onion
3 Tbl Flour
3.5 dl Milk
1 bag Mushroom Soup (for 1 l)
1 handfull Peas
1 cup crushed Potato Chips

Preparation:

Chop onion.

Fry onion in the butter for 3 minutes. Add flour. Add milk gradually and keep stirring.

Make up the mushroom soup with half the amount of water and add it to the Butter-Flour sauce.

Add the tuna and peas.

Fill a casserole with alternate layers of tuna mixture and crushed potato chips. End with a layer of potato chip crumbs on top.

Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.

Source:

Shirley Eigenmann from her time in Canada in the 1960s.
