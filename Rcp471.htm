Lemon and Herb Roasted Greek Lamb

Ingredients for 6-8 Persons:
1 de-boned leg of lamb
1/3 cup olive oil
1/4 cup Lemon juice
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
2 tablespoons crushed garlic
1/3 cup chopped oreganum
1/3 cup chopped rosemary
Black Pepper

Preparation:

Preparation time: 10 minutes. Cooking time: +/- 1 hour depending on mass of meat.

Using a sharp knife, butterfly the leg of lamb and cut a few slits in the meat. Whisk the remaining ingredients together, pour over the lamb and allow to marinate for 2-3 hours.

Remove the lamb from the marinade and place in an oven, preheated to 200°C.

Roasting time is calculated at roughly 15 minutes per 500g, plus an additional 10 minutes thereafter. Once ready, remove from oven and allow to stand for 10 minutes prior to carving. This allows the meat to relax and results in more tenderness.

Source:

Recipe Booklet from Checkers, Tastes of the World 2005
