Chicken Curry

Ingredients for 4-6
1 kg chicken pieces
2 Tsp Sunflower Oil
2 Onions
3 sticks Cinnamon
3 cardamom pods
1 fat garlic clove, crushed
1 chunk root ginger
3 ripe red tomatoes, skinned and chopped
2 Tsp mild curry powder or garam masala
6 curry leaves
salt
1 cup water or coconut milk
4 potatoes
fresh coriander leaves

Preparation:

Wash the chicken pieces. Chop the onions.Peel and grate the ginger.

Heat the oil in a heavy saucepan and braise the onions slowly together with the cinnamon and cracked cardamom pods, for about 10 minutes. Add the chicken pieces, garlic and ginger. Cover and cook for about 20 minutes, stirring now and again, until a good colour.

Add the tomatoes, curry powder or masala and curry leaves, and mix together well. Add some salt and pour in the water or coconut milk.

Cube the the potatoes and stirm them in, cooking gently for about 30 minutes or until everything is tender. stir in a good handful of chopped coriander leaves and check seasoning.

Serve with steamed basmati rice, or roti breads, a sweet chutney and a sharp mango pickle. Make a tomato sambal to go with it: a mix of chopped tomato, chopped onion and fresh chili, a little vinegar, and salt and pepper. Or a bowl of cool cucumber, chopped and mixed with yoghurt and mint, fresh or dried.

Cape Town Food, p88
