Sage and Onion Savoury Mince

Ingredients:
100 g minced beef
1 tsp oil
1 carrot
1.5 tbl sage and onion stuffing mix
2 tbl ketchup

Preparation:

Fry mince in hot oil until brown. Add stuffing mix with grated carrot and tomato purée. Add 4 spoons boiling water, stir well, cover and simmer for 35 minutes.

Serve with crisp salad.

Source:

Herbert Eigenmann, Code: SOM

Valuation:

A nice taste. I used flavoured croutins instead of the stuffing mix which is not obtainable here. RE 12.5.2010
