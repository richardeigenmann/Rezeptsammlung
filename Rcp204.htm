Bean and Tuna Salad

Zutaten für 4 Personen:
1 large can Tuna
3 Tl lemon juice
Freshly ground pepper
Pinch of Salt
115 g (4 oz) Haricot Beans
1 clove garlic
Parsley

Zubereitung:

Soak beans overnight - cook either in a preassure cooker or by boiling. Drain and cool.

Drain Tuna and put oil from the can into a salad bowl, add lemon juice, salt, pepper and finely chopped garlic.

Add beans and flaked Tuna, mix well.

Just before serving, add a generous amount of chopped parsley.

(A tasty and simple 'starter', wine vinegar can be added if lemon juice is too sharp a flavour.)
