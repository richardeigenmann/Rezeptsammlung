Spiced Pumpkin and Tomato Soup

Ingrediens for 8:
1 Onion
2 Tsp Sunflower Oil
1 Tsp Curry Powder
1 Clove Garlic
1 kg Pumpkin
5 cups Chicken or Vegetable Stock
1/2 - 1 cup Buttermilk (if cold) or Coconut Milk (if hot)
1 can Indian -style chopped tomatoes (410 g)
Salt and Pepper

Mise en place:

Chop onion

Peel and chop pumpkin into equally sized cubes

Peel the carlic clove

Preparation:

Cook the onion in oil with a little salt until softened but not browned. If necessary add a little water.

Stir in the curry powder and add the crushed garlic. Then add the diced pumpkin, cover and cook for about 10 minutes.

Pour in the stock, cover and simmer for about 30 minutes or until the pumpkin is tender.

Purée with an electric hand-held blender until smooth.

If serving cold, add enough buttermilk to make a suitable consistency. Stir in the tomatoes but do not purée. Check seasoning and refrigerate.

If serving hot, add the coconut milk, stir in the tomatoes without using the blender.

If you like them, sprinkle coriander leaves over the soup and sprinkle with garam masala.
