Pork Salad

Ingredients for 4 People:
300 g cooked leg of pork cold
1/2 cucumber
125 g yoghurt
1 EL chopped chives
1/4 TL dry mustard
1/4 TL garlic salt
1 TL lemon juice
4 lettuce leaves
1 tomato

Preparation:

Prepare dressing by mixing yoghurt, mustard, garlic salt, lemon juice and chopped chives together.

Cut the pork und cucumber in to small strips und mix together into a bowl.

Toss dressing lightly with the pork and cucumber und serve in cupped lettuce leaves. Garnishc with sliced tomato.

Source:

Herbert Eigenmann, Code: SWS
