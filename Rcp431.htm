Mini Cheesecakes

Serves 6-8
230 g Lancewood cream cheese (Philadelphia)
220 g Lancewood creamed cottage cheese (Rahmquark)
6-8 ginger biscuits
2 Eggs
100g Sugar
30 ml lemon juice
200 ml granadilla pulp (Fresh --> open) or Cherry Jam

Mise en place:

Preparation:

Place one ginger biscuit in the base of eash ramekin.

Blend the Cream Cheese, Creamed cottage cheese, eggs, sugar and lemon juice in a food processor until the mixture is smooth.

Fold in the granadilla pulp and divide between the ramekins.

Bake in a preheated oven for 25-30 minutes at 170°C or until the tops are firm. Serve room temperature or cold.

Variation:

Strawberry and Chocolate Mini Cheesecakes. Use chocolate coated digestive biscuits instead of ginger biscuits. Process a punnet of fresh strawberries or a can of drained strawberries until smooth to make up 200 ml of strawberry pulp. Use this to replace the granadilla pulp.

Valuation:

Finally got round to testing this since I didn't have any suitable dishes. Instead of ginger biscuit I used Willisauerringli which turned out very nicely too. The pudding was quite soft despite the top having hardened a little. There was no indication of the temperature. I used 180°C. I have corrected the recipe to 170. RE 22 Apr 2012
