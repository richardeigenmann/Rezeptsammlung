Spiced Lentil Dip

Ingredients for 8
1/2 cup brown lentils
1/2 cup red lentils
3 Tsp Olive Oil
1 Onion
2 fat Garlic cloves
1 Tsp Tumeric
1 Tsp ground Cumin
1/2 Cup Coriander Leaves
1 Tsp Lemon Juice
1/2 Tsp Chili Powder
Salt

Mise en place:

Soak the lentils in cold water for 20-30 minutes.

Mince onion

Preparation:

Skim off any gritty bits from the soaked lentils and drain them.

Cook lentils for 30 minutes or until tender.

Drain the lentils

Heat the oil in a wide, heavy pan. Add the onion and cook gently till softened but not browned.

Stir in the crushed garlic, tumeric and cumin until fragrant.

Process or blend together with the lentils, coriander, lemon juice and chili powder until smooth.

Add salt to taste and store in the fridge.

Source:

Cape Town Food, p 82

Valuation:

A very nice Dip.

We were not too impressed on 28 Feb 2010. It is an OK dip and gets eaten but there are better things out there. RE

Serving suggestion:

Serve at room temperature with crisply fried poppadums for dipping.
